Nicola Küng's Movie Reel

Filmmaking is a process. It takes many years to develop ones style. Looking back, it's easy to be ashamed of ones beginnings. I don't feel like this. That's why I proudly present my entire portfolio of films below. Reaching back all the way to my very first experiments with cameras. Enjoy the journey!

2019

ESF 2019 Aftermovie

Van Diego

My latest travel video. Shot during a ten-day road trip down the Californian Cost from San Francisco to San Diego.

Professional shoot for a Swiss legaltech startup. Involved everything from drawing a storyboard, conducting and editing interviews to flying a drone.

2018

Autonomous Christmas Lab

Professional shoot for my lab at uni. Only had one week for the whole job and have never been as stressed out as when all robot operators had to be performing for the same shot.

Ascento

Roll-Out video for a robotics project I worked on myself. Marked my first time shooting interviews with two cameras and working extensively with text. With around four days of production time probably my most stressful job ever.

2 Interrailin' Dudes

About Me

When I'm not making movies, I spend my time studying Mechanical Engineering in Zurich, Switzerland. Or the other way around. Making movies has been a passion of mine long before taking up my studies though. It all started when my god-mother gifted me a video camera for Christmas at the age of twelve. Since then, I have been observing the world through the lens. Fascinated more by the recording of events rather than the events themselves. Because filmmaking has the magical ability to shape every aspect of how we perceive something. And that's what I find so dope about it.
